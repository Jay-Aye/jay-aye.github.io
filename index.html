My Story

At 28 years old and 7 years experience in the surveillance/casino gaming industry, I decided it was time to make a career change and learn some new skills. I’ve always had a passion for creating and making things. My first exposure to coding was with Arduino when I copied and pasted a code from GitHub. My eyes were opened to a world of possibilities. From there it has been an adventure of epic proportions. Currently dualing with Ruby, HTML and CSS with future battles planned with JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, React and Node. Although still early days, the raw power and flexibility of back end constantly inspires me to push my boundaries in my learning. At the end of this fairy tail I want to work with a company that makes a difference in the world. Then we will all live happily ever after . . .
